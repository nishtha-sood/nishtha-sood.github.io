NYC Food Waste Analysis & Forecasting

Tools: R, Tableau, Poisson Regression, K-Means Clustering

This project analyzes 6,500+ rows of sanitation, demographic, and restaurant data from 2015–2025 and builds a predictive model (R² = 0.93) to forecast borough-level food waste in NYC. The analysis used clustering and regression to identify policy implications and restaurant density patterns.
